Check or Turn on Direct3D

Flight Simulator Configuration Help Center

Your video card must have at least the minimum amount of video memory that is required by the game or simulation. Make sure that the video adapter meets the minimum requirements. See the system requirements that are listed on the box or find your product and its requirements on the following Microsoft Web site:

http://www.microsoft.com/games/PC/gameslist_date.asp

To check or turn on Direct3D

  1. Click Start, and then click Run.
  2. Type dxdiag in the Open box, and then click OK. If you get a message asking if you want to proceed, click Yes.
  3. On the Display 1 tab, verify that all DirectX features are enabled. If any are disabled, click Enable next to the feature.
  4. Click Exit.
