Adjust Hardware Graphics Acceleration

Flight Simulator Configuration Help Center

To adjust hardware acceleration

  1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
  2. Double-click Display.
  3. On the Settings tab, click Advanced.
  4. Click the Troubleshoot tab.
  5. Move the Hardware Acceleration slider two notches to the left of Full.
  6. Click OK, and then click OK again.

NOTE: If your game runs with video acceleration turned down, it may indicate that you need to update your video driver or that your video card cannot run your game at full acceleration.
