Adjust Display Settings

Flight Simulator Configuration Help Center

This product requires a video card and monitor that can run a resolution of at least 1024 x 768 at High Color (16 bit). Set your Windows display resolution to 1024 x 768 at High Color (16 bit), and then test the product.

To change your computer's display

  1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
  2. Double-click Display.
  3. On the Settings tab, drag the Screen resolution slider right to 1024 by 768 or higher.
  4. In the Color quality list, select 16 bit or 32 bit.
  5. Click OK.
  6. Click Yes to accept the settings.

NOTE: After changing your display resolution, you may have to adjust your monitor settings so that your screen displays correctly. While these adjustments are often possible by using the controls found on the front of your monitor, review your monitor documentation for more information.

If you know what resolution your game is set to run at, set your Windows display settings to the same resolution as the game. If Windows won't work with that resolution then the program probably won't either.
